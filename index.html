How The Game Works

You represent the Human race and are playing cards against the Martians to try to save the earth. Hit the start button to start the game, hit the next button to deal the next set of cards. If the Martians score is higher, they get a point. If the Humans score is higher, we get a point. And if its a tie, then nobody gets a point. Once the deck finishes, the winner will be announced. Good Luck! Save Earth!
